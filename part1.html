The Cassini-Huygens Spacecraft

Mission Summary

Cassini-Huygens is the first unmanned spacecraft to orbit the planet Saturn and it is the fourth spacecraft to visit Saturn. Cassini arrived in 2004 and its mission is ongoing as of October 2015. Cassini is designed to carry-out a wide range of research during it's mission, making it the second-largest unmanned space probe ever built.

Spacecraft Design

Cassini is equipped with four different imaging devices, six different field, particle, and wave detectors, and two microwave sensing devices including a radar.
